Xu He

Where Do Machine and Brain Intersect?

I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Jacobs University and a Research Associate at MINDS. My academic interests span from machine learning to computational neuroscience. Currently I am developing mathematical models and algorithms that can overcome challenges facing unconventional neuromorphic hardware, such as computation with spikes, device instability and catastrophic forgetting.

NeuRam Cube Project
